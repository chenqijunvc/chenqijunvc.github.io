## About Vince Qijun Chen
关于 陈祺俊

Hello, I'm **Vince (Qijun) Chen, CFA**, and this is my personal platform to share insights at the dynamic intersection of **finance, technology, and strategic thinking**.

您好，我是**陈祺俊 (Vince Qijun Chen), CFA**，这是我分享对**金融、科技和战略思维**之间动态交集见解的个人平台。

With nearly a decade of experience as a **portfolio manager and quantitative researcher**, I've dedicated my career to exploring innovative approaches to capital markets. My expertise spans:

凭借近十年的**投资组合管理和量化研究**经验，我致力于探索资本市场的创新方法。我的专长包括：

* **Quantitative Investment Strategies:** Designing and implementing data-driven approaches to asset management, including a deep focus on **Free Cash Flow Quality (FCFQM)**.
    **量化投资策略：** 设计和实施数据驱动的资产管理方法，包括对**自由现金流质量模型（FCFQM）**的深入关注。
* **ML & AI in Finance:** Applying cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques for enhanced alpha capture, risk management, and predictive analytics.
    **金融领域的机器学习与人工智能：** 应用尖端人工智能和机器学习技术，以增强阿尔法捕获、风险管理和预测分析。
* **Asset Allocation:** Developing strategic frameworks for diversified portfolio construction across various asset classes.
    **资产配置：** 为不同资产类别的多元化投资组合构建制定战略框架。
* **ETF Investing:** Expertise in the structure, selection, and management of Exchange Traded Funds for efficient portfolio implementation.
    **ETF投资：** 精通交易所交易基金（ETF）的结构、选择和管理，以实现高效的投资组合实施。

Currently, I serve as **Vice President of Public Equity & Portfolio Management at Abacus Life, Inc.** (parent company of Abacus FCF Advisors LLC). In this capacity, I am a Portfolio Manager for several prominent ETFs and SMAs, and am jointly and primarily responsible for their day-to-day management:

目前，我担任**Abacus Life, Inc.（Abacus FCF Advisors LLC 的母公司）的上市股票与投资组合管理副总裁**。在此职位上，我是多只知名ETF和独立管理账户（SMA）的投资组合经理，并共同负责其日常管理：

* [Abacus FCF Leaders ETF (ABFL)](https://www.nasdaq.com/market-activity/etf/abfl)
* [Abacus FCF International Leaders ETF (ABLG)](https://www.nasdaq.com/market-activity/etf/ablg)
* [Abacus FCF Real Assets Leaders ETF (ABLD)](https://www.nasdaq.com/market-activity/etf/abld)
* [Abacus FCF Innovation Leaders ETF (ABOT)](https://www.nasdaq.com/market-activity/etf/abot)
* [Abacus FCF Small Cap Leaders ETF (ABLS)](https://www.nasdaq.com/market-activity/etf/abls)

My aim with this blog is to go beyond traditional finance, offering **thought leadership and practical perspectives** on how emerging technologies and analytical rigor are reshaping industries. Here, you'll find a blend of in-depth research, personal reflections, and forward-looking discussions.

我创建此博客的目的是超越传统金融，就新兴技术和分析严谨性如何重塑行业提供**思想领导力和实践观点**。在这里，您将找到深入研究、个人反思和前瞻性讨论的融合。

## My Journey & Credentials
我的历程与资质

My professional journey began at FCF Advisors (formerly TrimTabs Asset Management), where I progressed from Quantitative Analyst to Director of Research. This experience solidified my passion for leveraging **data science, equity valuation, and computer programming** to drive **alpha capture research** and **systematic equity portfolio management**.

我的职业生涯始于FCF Advisors（前身为TrimTabs Asset Management），从量化分析师晋升为研究总监。这段经历巩固了我利用**数据科学、股票估值和计算机编程**进行**阿尔法捕获研究**和**系统化股票投资组合管理**的热情。

I hold a **Master of Science in Quantitative Methods and Modeling** from Baruch College and a **Bachelor of Economics** from Guangdong University of Foreign Studies. As a **CFA Charterholder** and an active member of the **CQF Institute New York Society**, I am committed to continuous learning and upholding the highest industry standards. My **Salesforce AI Specialist Certification** further underscores my dedication to understanding and applying AI in practical contexts.

我拥有巴鲁克学院的**量化方法与建模理学硕士学位**和广东外语外贸大学的**经济学学士学位**。作为一名**特许金融分析师（CFA Charterholder）**和**CQF协会纽约分会的活跃成员**，我致力于持续学习并维护最高的行业标准。我的**Salesforce AI专家认证**进一步突显了我对理解和应用人工智能的奉献。

Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy staying active with **soccer, water sports, and cycling**. I also value quality time spent with my **family and friends.**

在工作之余，我喜欢通过**足球、水上运动和骑行**保持活力。我也珍视与**家人和朋友**共度的时光。
