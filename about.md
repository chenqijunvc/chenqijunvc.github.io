## About Vince Qijun Chen

Hello, I'm **Vince (Qijun) Chen, CFA**, a leading **quantitative finance expert** and **portfolio manager** specializing in the transformative application of **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **data science** to capital markets. With nearly a decade of experience, I guide investors through the complex intersection of finance and cutting-edge technology.

My career has been dedicated to exploring innovative approaches to **asset management** and **alpha capture**. My expertise spans:

* **Quantitative Investment Strategies:** Designing and implementing data-driven approaches to **systematic equity portfolio management**, with a deep focus on **Free Cash Flow Quality (FCFQM)** and advanced **financial modeling**.
* **AI & Machine Learning in Finance:** Applying cutting-edge **artificial intelligence** and **machine learning (ML)** techniques for enhanced **predictive analytics**, **risk management**, and **algorithmic trading strategies**.
* **Strategic Asset Allocation:** Developing robust frameworks for diversified **portfolio construction** across various asset classes, optimized for performance and risk.
* **ETF Investing & Management:** Extensive expertise in the structure, selection, and day-to-day management of **Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs)** for efficient and scalable portfolio implementation.

Currently, I serve as **Vice President of Public Equity & Portfolio Management at Abacus Life, Inc.** (parent company of Abacus FCF Advisors LLC). In this capacity, I am a Portfolio Manager for several prominent **Abacus FCF Leaders ETFs** and SMAs, and am jointly and primarily responsible for their day-to-day management:

* [Abacus FCF Real Assets Leaders ETF (ABLD)](https://www.morningstar.com/etfs/bats/abld/quote)
* [Abacus FCF Small Cap Leaders ETF (ABLS)](https://www.morningstar.com/etfs/bats/abls/quote)
* [Abacus FCF International Leaders ETF (ABLG)](https://www.morningstar.com/etfs/bats/ablg/quote)
* [Abacus Flexible Bond Leaders ETF (ABXB)](https://www.morningstar.com/etfs/bats/abxb/quote)

## My Journey & Credentials

My professional journey began at FCF Advisors (formerly TrimTabs Asset Management), where I progressed from Quantitative Analyst to Director of Research. This experience solidified my passion for leveraging **data science**, **equity valuation**, and **computer programming** to drive **alpha capture research** and **systematic equity portfolio management** powered by data-driven insights.

I hold a **Master of Science in Quantitative Methods and Modeling** from Baruch College and a **Bachelor of Economics** from Guangdong University of Foreign Studies. As a **CFA Charterholder** and an active member of the **CQF Institute New York Society**, I am committed to continuous learning and upholding the highest industry standards in quantitative finance. My **Salesforce AI Specialist Certification** further underscores my dedication to understanding and applying **artificial intelligence** in practical financial contexts, enhancing **data analytics** and **predictive modeling**.

Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy staying active with **soccer, water sports, and cycling**. I also value quality time spent with my **family and friends.**
