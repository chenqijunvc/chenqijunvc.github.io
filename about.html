I'm Vince Qijun Chen, a portfolio manager and quantamental strategist specializing in systematic investing. My work focuses on bridging institutional-grade analysis with accessible investment solutions, developing innovative ETF strategies, and sharing market insights on financial media.

Professional Background

My career centers on developing quantamental investment strategies that combine rigorous quantitative analysis with fundamental research. I specialize in free cash flow analysis and have pioneered systematic approaches to quality investing, small cap selection, and innovation investing through ETF strategies.

ETF Strategy & Portfolio Management

FCF-ROIC Methodology

A proprietary systematic approach that combines Free Cash Flow and Return on Invested Capital for quality company selection.

Quantamental Analysis

Developing systematic frameworks that blend rigorous quantitative screening with deep fundamental research to uncover alpha.

Systematic Risk Management

Implementing disciplined portfolio construction and risk controls to optimize risk-adjusted returns across market cycles.

Investment Philosophy

My approach focuses on identifying companies that generate substantial cash flow and deploy capital efficiently. Whether addressing the quality investing challenge, avoiding zombie companies in small caps, or finding profitable innovation, I believe in systematic, measurable approaches to investment selection.
